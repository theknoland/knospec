Identities management.

## guest 

`guest` is Knoland's anonymous identity. Contributions made by `guest` are not tracked and rewarded. Copyrights of guest contents belong to Knoland.

`guest` has no profile.

## Fuzzy profile

To protect the privacy of and provide personalized services to Knolanders at the same time, profile attributes maybe approximately true. For example, to provide mapping service to Knolanders, address of a nearby public institution maybe used instead of the true address. 

The truthfulness of the profile attributes must be disclosed to Knoland.

## True profile

Profile with at least one true attribute is considered a true identity. Knolander must maintain at least one true identity.  

## Multiple profiles

Knolanders may have multiple profiles, each correspond to one persona. Fake profiles must be linked to a true profile.
