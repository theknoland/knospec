## The Land

The land on which human minds dwell is under attack, by no other but humankind.

While natural things grow under constraints, beautiful things emerge out of pains, harmful things as the result of abundance:

- Advertisement. 
- Games.
- Language.


## No Land

If I tell you there is a piece of land on the web that has no advertisement, no AI generated content, no digital games, well trafficked and is profitable, you would say to me: no, there is no such land. 


## Adding 'K'

'K' for knowledge.

Youtube and TikTok for videos, Wikipedia, WhatsApp and Twitter for text, Spotify for music, Instagram for pictures... For all modalities of artifacts and each of their many forms there are more than one dedicated platforms, except knowledge. 

## The Knoland

Like beehives for the bees and anthills for the ants, Knoland is the kingdom of inquisitive minds.
