The Knoble, Knoland's Bible, is based on a fiction. It's a vehicle carrying Knolanders' collective wisdom. It's sacred nonetheless, even though we don't claim that it's real.

It's a tale of two civilizations. Base II, the distant alien civilization has already gone through what Base X, the earth, is going and about to go through. On the verge of extinction, Base II searched for their next home and set their eyes on Base X.

So the story begins ...
