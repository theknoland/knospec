## Vision

To sustain and advance humanity.

## Mission

Enhance human's living experiences by digitizing and turning human knowledge into power.

## Ideology

Knoland is a Kult.

Knolanders adhere to Kommunism, a living pragmatic ideology constantly taking the good parts of Communitarianism, Communalism, and you read it correct, Communism, as well as any other-isms of the past, present and future.

- From Communism:
  - Abolition of private knowledge, without encroaching on privacy.

## Values

Knolanders are different.

We believe:

- Privacy is an inalienable right
- Free is not right
- Open has nothing to hide

and in practice:

- We fight for privacy
  - We don't physically track our users in the first place, as far as secular laws allow.
- We are for profit but not greedy
  - We use profitability as the yardstick of the value provided by our products and services.
  - We charge secular institutions for our products and services which are priced to be minimally profitable
  - We share our profit among Knolanders according to their individual contribution.
  - We hate with passion and don't allow unsolicited advertisement.
- We are radically open
  - Our books are open. Everyone is encouraged to audit our bank statements.
  - Our code is transparent: open for examination and contribution but closed for modification and commercialization.
  - Our decisions are openly made through democratic processes such as request for comments (RFC), request for implementation (RFI), and voting.
